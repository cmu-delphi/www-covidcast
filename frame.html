About COVIDcast

COVIDcast displays signals related to COVID-19 activity levels across the U.S., derived from a variety of anonymized, aggregated data sources made available to us by multiple partners. Each signal may reflect the prevalence of COVID-19 infection, mild symptoms or more severe disease, over time, in the finest-possible geographic resolutions: U.S. county, metropolitan area and hospital region. Learn more about our methodology.

All these signals taken together may suggest heightened or rising COVID-19 activity in specific locations, information critical to public health officials. Pandemic forecasting data coming soon.
